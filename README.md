# 🌍 ClimateLensAfrica

### Seeing Climate. Sharing Solutions. Shaping Africa's Future.

ClimateLensAfrica is an African climate education and storytelling platform dedicated to making environmental knowledge accessible, engaging and relevant.

Through climate education, storytelling, visual media and practical solutions, ClimateLensAfrica explores the environmental challenges shaping Africa and the world.

---

## 🌱 Our Mission

To help people understand climate change, environmental protection and sustainability through powerful information, storytelling and African perspectives.

---

## 🌍 What We Explore

- 🌡️ Climate Change
- 🌦️ Weather and Climate
- 🌱 Conservation
- 🌾 Climate-Smart Agriculture
- 💧 Water Security
- 🐘 Biodiversity and Wildlife
- ⚡ Renewable Energy
- 🍎 Food Systems and Food Waste
- 🌳 Forests and Ecosystems
- 🌍 African Climate Solutions

---

## ✨ Features

## 🎯 Vision

Climate change is not a distant story.

It is affecting communities, farmers, ecosystems and economies across Africa today.

ClimateLensAfrica exists to help people see these changes, understand them and discover the solutions that can shape a more sustainable future.

---

## 🎥 Storytelling Through Film

ClimateLensAfrica combines environmental education with visual storytelling, documentary filmmaking and voice-over narration.

Because sometimes the most powerful way to teach people about climate change is not through statistics alone.

It is through a story.
