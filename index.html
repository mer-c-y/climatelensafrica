Why ClimateLensAfrica?

Climate change is not a distant story. It is already affecting communities, farmers, ecosystems and economies across Africa.

ClimateLensAfrica exists to make climate knowledge accessible, engaging and relevant to African communities. Through storytelling, education, visual media and practical solutions, we explore the environmental challenges shaping our present and future.
