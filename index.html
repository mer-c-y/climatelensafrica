ABOUT CLIMATELENSAFRICA

Seeing Climate.
Sharing Solutions.
Shaping Africa's Future.

Climate change is not a distant story. It is already affecting communities, farmers, ecosystems and economies across Africa.

ClimateLensAfrica exists to make climate knowledge accessible, engaging and relevant through education, storytelling, visual media and practical solutions.

Because sometimes the most powerful way to teach people about climate change is not through statistics alone.

It is through a story.
